# Project Charter — Aroma Lab

## 1. Project Title
**Aroma Lab — Integrated Coffee Chain Management System**

## 2. Problem Statement
Aroma Lab is a growing coffee chain with 6 branches in Almaty, Kazakhstan. Currently, all business processes are handled manually:
- Sales are recorded on paper or standalone cash registers
- Inventory is counted manually at end of day
- Invoices are created by an accountant from paper receipts
- No real-time visibility into KPIs (revenue, stock levels, food cost)

This leads to **data entry errors**, **stockouts**, **delayed invoicing**, and **inability to make data-driven decisions** across branches.

## 3. Proposed IT Solution
A web-based ERP system that unifies Point-of-Sale, Inventory Management, and Financial Accounting into a single application. The system will automate stock deduction upon sale, auto-generate fiscal invoices, and provide real-time KPI dashboards.

## 4. SMART Goals

| Component | Goal |
|-----------|------|
| **Specific** | Build a web-based management system covering sales (POS), inventory tracking, and financial reporting for a multi-branch coffee chain |
| **Measurable** | Reduce manual data entry by 80%; achieve real-time stock alerts; auto-generate 100% of invoices |
| **Achievable** | Use React + Node.js + SQLite stack; team of 1-2 developers; leverage existing database schema |
| **Relevant** | Addresses real operational pain points of a growing coffee chain facing scalability challenges |
| **Time-bound** | Complete MVP within 6 weeks (March 15 – April 29, 2025) |
